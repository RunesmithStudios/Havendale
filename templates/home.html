The "heroes"

Oathbreaker The Stone Speaker Born in the hellish northeast of the Damned Land, Oathbreaker started his career as a professional traitor when his best friend threw a rock at him and that stone told him to leave his friend. Forever. Thus becoming The Stone Speaker. The whereabouts of the sad friend are unknown.

Scream The Forge Augur Some say he is a prophet, others, a con artist. All we know is he saw some serious shit. And like every good prophet in myth, he definitely wasn't in his right mind when he did.

Synapse The Geometer Sick and tired of small countries, Synapse came to the Damned Land hoping to measure the earth. However his fellow smiths lost the measuring devices and still haven't told him about it.

Ventorim The Outlander Born in the Damned Land, unable to cope with his origins, Ventorim sought acceptance of the People from Beyond, calling himself The Outlander. He failed.
